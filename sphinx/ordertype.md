## Orders Supported

Basic order types. See [IB Doc](http://interactivebrokers.github.io/tws-api/basic_orders.html) for details.

* Auction
* Auction Limit
* Market
* Market If Touched
* Market On Close
* Market On Open
* Market to Limit
* Limit Order
* Limit if Touched
* Limit on Close
* Limit on Open
* Stop
* Stop Limit
* Trailing Stop
* Trailing Stop Limit
